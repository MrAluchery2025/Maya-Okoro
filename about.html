About Maya

Maya Okoro is a visionary fashion designer committed to creating sustainable, nature-inspired fashion. With roots deeply connected to traditional African textile techniques and modern design sensibilities, Maya brings authenticity, elegance, and environmental consciousness into every collection.

Her journey began with a love for storytelling through fabric — weaving together culture, sustainability, and creativity. Maya’s work has been featured in eco-fashion runways worldwide and continues to inspire a movement toward ethical fashion.
